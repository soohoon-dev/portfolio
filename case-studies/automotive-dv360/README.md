## ANALYZE: Performance Deep Dive

### 1. Programmatic Display: Sales Campaign Evolution

**Comparison:** Dec 2021 (29d) vs Jul 2025 (21d)

#### 2021 Baseline: Getaway Sales (Broad Reach Strategy)

- Budget: $17,726
- Impressions: 14.8M
- Clicks: 26,807
- CTR: **0.18%**
- CPC: **$0.66**
- CPM: $1.20

#### 2025 Optimized: Summer Sales Hybrid (Precision Strategy)

- Budget: $5,499 (**69% cost reduction**)
- Impressions: 2.8M (focused audience)
- Clicks: 107,135 (**4x increase**)
- CTR: **3.86%** (+2,044%)
- CPC: **$0.05** (-92%)
- CPM: $1.98

**Strategic Insight:**
The 81% reduction in impressions wasn't a failure‚Äîit represented eliminating low-quality inventory. By focusing on high-propensity audiences, we achieved a 21x CTR improvement while reducing cost per engagement by 92%. The hybrid vehicle's smaller addressable market actually benefited from precision targeting.

**Hypothesis Validation:** ‚úÖ H1 confirmed‚Äîprecision targeting dramatically improved engagement metrics while reducing waste.

---

### 2. DV360 CTV: Scale + Efficiency

**Comparison:** May 2024 vs Jul 2025 Launch (14d, ~$1,820 budget)

| Metric              | 2024 Santa Fe | 2025 IONIQ 9 | Change     |
| ------------------- | ------------- | ------------ | ---------- |
| **Impressions**     | 205,190       | 287,101      | **+40%**   |
| **Complete Views**  | 202,060       | 281,747      | **+39%**   |
| **Completion Rate** | 98%           | 98%          | Maintained |
| **CPM**             | $8.87         | $6.34        | **-28%**   |
| **CPC (reference)** | $606          | $202         | -67%       |

**Strategic Insight:**
By negotiating better CPM rates and optimizing inventory sources, we maintained the same $1,820 budget while achieving 40% more reach. The sustained 98% completion rate demonstrates that efficiency gains didn't compromise audience quality‚Äîviewers remained highly engaged.

**Hypothesis Validation:** ‚úÖ H2 confirmed‚ÄîCTV delivered both scale and efficiency improvements.

### 3. YouTube Video: Cost Efficiency at Scale

**Comparison A:** Launch Campaigns (14d, ~$3,940 budget)

| Metric              | 2024 Santa Fe | 2025 IONIQ 9 | Change   |
| ------------------- | ------------- | ------------ | -------- |
| **Views**           | 265,701       | 369,520      | **+39%** |
| **View Rate**       | 61.74%        | 63.19%       | +1.5pp   |
| **CPV**             | $0.02         | $0.01        | **-50%** |
| **CPM**             | $9.16         | $6.74        | **-26%** |
| **100% Completion** | 62.72%        | 63.26%       | +0.5pp   |

**Comparison B:** Sales Campaigns (29d)

| Metric              | Dec 2021 | Jul 2025 | Change    |
| ------------------- | -------- | -------- | --------- |
| **Views**           | 372,516  | 700,601  | **+88%**  |
| **View Rate**       | 47.16%   | 61.58%   | **+14pp** |

**Strategic Insight:**
YouTube's auction improvements and our refined audience targeting delivered a double win: halving the cost per view while dramatically improving view quality. The 14-point increase in completion rate (47% ‚Üí 62%) indicates we're reaching more engaged audiences who watch the entire ad.

### 4. Paid Search: Quality Over Volume

**Comparison:** Dec 2021 vs Jul 2025 (29d)

| Metric          | 2021    | 2025   | Change      |
| --------------- | ------- | ------ | ----------- |
| **Clicks**      | 2,184   | 1,177  | -46%        |
| **Impressions** | 620,322 | 21,630 | **-96.5%**  |
| **CTR**         | 0.35%   | 5.44%  | **+1,454%** |
| **CPC**         | $0.49   | $1.41  | +188%       |
| **CPM**         | $1.74   | $76.88 | N/A         |

**Strategic Insight:**
The massive impression drop reflects a deliberate shift from broad match keywords to exact/phrase match high-intent queries. While CPC tripled, CTR improved 15x‚Äîmeaning we're paying more per click but getting dramatically better qualified traffic. The reduced click volume (2,184 ‚Üí 1,177) represents eliminating tire-kickers in favor of in-market shoppers.

**Hypothesis Validation:** ‚úÖ H3 confirmed‚Äîhigh-intent focus improved CTR substantially despite higher CPC.

## ACT: Strategic Takeaways & Next Steps

### Proven Optimization Strategies

1. **Display Campaigns:** Precision targeting (contextual, behavioral, 1P audiences) can deliver 20x+ CTR improvements while reducing cost per engagement
2. **CTV Integration:** Maintains premium completion rates (98%) while offering 28% CPM improvements versus early adoption
3. **YouTube Optimization:** Audience refinement + creative testing can halve CPV while improving completion rates
4. **Search Refinement:** High-intent keyword focus trades volume for quality‚Äîcritical for high-consideration purchases like vehicles
